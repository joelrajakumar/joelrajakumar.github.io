I am a PhD candidate in computer science at the University of Maryland, advised by Yi-Kai Liu and Michael Gullans, affliliated with the Joint Center for Quantum Information and Computer Science (QuICS) and the Institute for Robust Quantum Simulation (RQS).

A broad research motivation of mine is to probe aspects of many-body physics through a computational complexity lens. I am especially interested in models of current quantum computers, such as noisy, random, and/or shallow circuits, and in quantum algorithms proposed for the next generation of quantum computers.
